Welcome to the ArmA 3 C2 Server

We focus on squad and helicopter-based tactical operations.
Our 10-slot missions are based on intelligence, communication and cooperation.
We have clear roles and responsibilities, and balance fun with structure.

Read More

Roles


[MC] Mission Command

Accountable for the success or failure of the designated mission

[PC] Platoon Commander

Responsible for actions of in-field fire teams / directs tactical flow of battle operations

[AC] Airborne Commander

Responsible for all airborne activities including troop resupply, troop movements and provisioning of supplies

[FTL1 / FTL2 / FTLn] Fire Team Lead

Frontline fire team leader responsible for execution of mission objectives, under direct control of the Platoon Commander

[AU1 / AUn] Airborne Unit

Additional airborne assets under direct control of the Airborne Commander, assisting with logistical and troop resupply actions

Our Current Deployment

We are currently engaged in the liberation of Lythium Province, Afghanistan. The mission itself is a slightly modified version of KP Liberation.

The server was set up with the aim of testing and developing the C2 Command and Control mod (by Mad Cheese). In addition, the server heavily uses addtional mods such as: AIC (Advanced Infantry Command), AIS (Alternative Injury System) and indiCam (by Woofer).
